What is NBIMUN 2024?

NBIMUN, short for North Bengal International Model United Nations, is an esteemed Model United Nations conference that brings together students from across the globe to engage in simulated diplomatic negotiations and debates. Organized in Rangpur, Bangladesh, NBIMUN provides a platform for young minds to delve into pressing global issues, emulate the workings of the United Nations, and foster cross-cultural understanding and collaboration.
